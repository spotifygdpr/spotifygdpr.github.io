Updates to our GDPR and Privacy Policy
Please read the following information then log in at the bottom of the email to approve these changes.
With the EU's General Data Protection Regulations (GDPR) coming in effect this May 25, 2018 you have probably received dozens of emails regarding updates of privacy policy and now it's our turn to bug you with this. Don't worry we will keep it short.

We're making a few changes and released our cookie and privacy policy to be compliant with the new regulations. Although these changes are spurred by European law, we think privacy is super important and so these changes will apply to everyone, no matter where you're located.

What has changed? Basically nothing for you except that we now provide more clarity about the uses of cookies with our Cookie Policy and how we use your data with our Privacy Policy.

We have also updated our support page on the website with a new option for any questions you may have regarding the privacy policy and data use of your account. Our Data Privacy Officer will be able to assist you with any concerns you may have.

Best Regards, Spotify
